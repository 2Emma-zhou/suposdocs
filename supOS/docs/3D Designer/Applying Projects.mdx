You can display the designed scene on third-party systems or on supOS.
## Display on third-party system

<ol>
<li>Design the scene and preview it.</li>
<li>Copy the page link during preview.</li>
<li>Change part of the link from <code>/editor/scenePrev?sceneId=xxx</code> to <code>/nest/scene?sceneName=your_scene_name</code>.</li>
<li>Iframe the URL to third-party systems.</li>
</ol>

## Display on supOS

<ol>
<li>Design the scene and preview it.</li>
<li>Copy the page link during preview.</li>
<li>Change part of the link from <code>/editor/scenePrev?sceneId=xxx</code> to <code>/nest/scene?sceneName=your_scene_name</code>.</li>
<li>Go to <b>Business Designer</b>, enter an app and drag a <b>Programmable Component</b> to the page and iframe the URL inside the script.

```js
<iframe
        id='iframe1'
        src='http://ip:port/apps/bluetron-3dedit/3DEDIT/#/nest/scene?sceneName=your_scene_name'
        style={{ width: '100%', height: '100%' }}
    />
```

</li>
</ol>
