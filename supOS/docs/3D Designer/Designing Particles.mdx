## Adding Particle
1. Select **APP Management** > **3D Designer** > **Particle Repository**, and then click **Add Particle**.
2. Select **Target category** and enter the particle name, and then click **Confirm**.


## Designing the Particle
1. Click <img width={25} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/3dDesigner/6.png"/>to edit this particle.
2. Set particle parameters on the right side.
