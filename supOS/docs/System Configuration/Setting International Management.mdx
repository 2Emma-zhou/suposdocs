You can upload language packages to the sopOS platform , and then switch the displayed language.

:::info
Presently, the supOS platform only supports simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, nglish and Indonesia.
:::

1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png"/> at the upper-right corner to go to the design center.
2. Click the <strong>International Management</strong> tab, and then click <strong>Language Settings</strong>.
3. Enable languages you need, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.

:::info
The enabled languages are displayed on the login page.
:::
4. Click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/23.png"/>, and then select <strong>Download Template</strong>.
5. Fill in the template as required, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/23.png"/> again, select <strong>Quick Import</strong>.

:::tip
You can also click <strong>New</strong> to add language translation one by one.
:::

<img width={450} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/57.png"/>

:::tip
To update the current translation, click <strong>Export</strong>, and then change on the exported file and import again. Click <strong>Edit</strong> under <strong>Operation</strong> to update the translation one by one.
:::
