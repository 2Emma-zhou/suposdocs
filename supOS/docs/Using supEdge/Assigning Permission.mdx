Assign permission of the pages you designed to different users, and then the pages can be displayed on the operation center according to granted permissions.
1. Log in to X-Edge, and then click <b>Designer</b> at the lower-left corner to access the design center.
2. Select <b>User Security</b> > <b>User Manage</b> on the left panel, and then click <b>Add</b>.
3. Enter the basic information of the user, and then click <b>Submit</b>.
![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supEdge/11.png)

4. Click **Permission** of the user you added, and then select pages to assign permissions.
![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supEdge/13.png)

5. Go to the operation center, select the pages you have permissions to and view the minitoring video.
![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supEdge/14.png)
