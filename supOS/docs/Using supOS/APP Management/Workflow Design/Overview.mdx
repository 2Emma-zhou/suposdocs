## Introduction

Workflow refers to the standardized process developed for a series of continuous and regular activities in the enterprise.

## Feature

<ul>
        <li>
          Qualified for multi-business scenarios, and complex industrial
          business process flows.
        </li>
        <li>Flexible multi-form customization.</li>
        <li>Cross-page data transfer and modification.</li>
        <li>Categorizes workflows by bound labels.</li>
        <li>
          Process-driven approach to realize enterprise closed-loop management.
        </li>
        <li>
          Multiple flow modes, rich task and event node types, and multiple
          administrator setting methods, support co-organization.
        </li>
        <li>
          Process management functions such as status monitoring, process log,
          to-do and to-read monitoring, and timing tasks.
        </li>
      </ul>
