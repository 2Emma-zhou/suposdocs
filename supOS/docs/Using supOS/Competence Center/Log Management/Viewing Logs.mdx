

1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png"/> at the upper-right corner to go to the design center.
2. Select <strong>Log Management</strong>.
3. Select log types from the left menu, and then set filters to check the corresponding logs.
:::info
For **Login Log**, under <strong>Verification Center</strong>, <strong>Current System</strong> stands for general login while <strong>Third Party Authentication</strong> means logging in to supOS through third party authorization centers.
:::
