### Adding Organization

1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/7.png" /> to go to the design center.
2. Select <strong>Organizational Structure</strong> > <strong>Organization Management</strong> > <strong>Department</strong>.
3. Select a company or a department, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/26.png"/>.
:::info
Click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/21.png"/> next to a company to switch between parent company and subsidiaries.
:::
4. Enter information of the department, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.

<img width={350} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/22.png"/>

5. Click <strong>Post</strong> tab, select a company or post, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/26.png"/>.
6. Enter post information, select a department to which the post belongs, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.

<img width={350} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/23.png"/>

:::info

<ul>
<li>You cannot delete department or post bound with personnels, nor default company.</li>
<li>Import data is for the selected company or department only.</li>
<li>When department code exists in the current company or department, the corresponding department will be updated from the import; when department code exists in other company or department, import will fail.
</li>
<li>When importing posts, make sure the upper level post codes exist.</li>
</ul>

::: 

7. (optional) Select a department or post, under <strong>Association Information</strong> > <strong>Related Role</strong>, you can view the linked post or staff and
link roles to post for corresponding permissions.
:::tip
Drag department or post to adjust their hierarchy.
:::
