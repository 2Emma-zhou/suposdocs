### Adding Staff

All staff needs to bind with posts. A staff can be bound with multiple companies, departments and posts. During login, you can select which company to log in to, and after login, you can switch companies by clicking company name at the upper-left corner.

1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/7.png" alt="" /> to go to the design center.
2. Select <strong>Organizational Structure</strong> > <strong>Personnel Management</strong> > <strong>Post</strong>.
3. Select a post, and then click <strong>New </strong>on the right side.
4. Enter information of the staff, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.
:::tip
Select <strong>Create User Account</strong> to set a username and password for the staff.
:::
 
<img width={350} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/28.png"/>

5. (optional) Click <strong>Department</strong> tab, select a company or department, and then you can export staff under.

<img width={350} src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/factoryModeling/29.png"/>

6. (optional) Click <strong>Template</strong> at the bottom, fill in the template and then click <strong>Import</strong> to import staff to the supOS platform in batches.

:::info
Import staff with the same code as one already exists on the post, the staff information will be updated but post will be added.
::: 

7. (optional) Select a post, and then click <strong>Transfer In</strong> to select staff to be transferred in to the post and set whether the post is the main post of the staff.

:::info
- After transfer in, you can click <strong>Transfer Out</strong> to transfer the staff out.
- If a staff is bound with a user, and the staff has resigned, you cannot view information of the staff and the bound user.
:::
