- <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/monitoring/51.png"/>: Adjust the video angle. Click it and then adjust the PTZ angle, focal length and turning speed on the pop-up window. <br />

### Video playback

Click <strong>Playback</strong> at the upper-right corner of the video window.

:::info
Make sure you have configured video collector and video source for <strong>Playback</strong> in business designer.
:::

<ul>
<li>
<img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/monitoring/55.png"/>: Adjust the play speed and play the video forward or backward.
</li>
<li>
<img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/monitoring/56.png"/>: Select a time period to play videos during that period.
</li>
<li>
<img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/monitoring/50.png"/>: Take a snapshot of the current image and save it to local computer.
