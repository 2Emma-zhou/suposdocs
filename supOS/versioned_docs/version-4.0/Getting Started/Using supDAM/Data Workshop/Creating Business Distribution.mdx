Create business section and subject domain to easily manage complex data.

1. Log in to supOS, and then select **Data Intelligence** > **Data Workshop** > **Data modeling** > **Business distribution**.
2. Click ![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supDAM/34.png), and then select **Business section** to add a business section.
3. Click ![](https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/supDAM/34.png) again, and then select **Subject domain**.
4. Enter the domain information, and then click **Confirm**.
