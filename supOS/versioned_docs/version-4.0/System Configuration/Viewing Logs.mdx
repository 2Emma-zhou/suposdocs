1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png"/> at the upper-right corner to go to the design center.
2. Select <strong>System Management</strong> > <strong>Log Management</strong> > <strong>Login Log</strong>.
3. Set filters and then click <strong>Search</strong>.

<ul>
<ul>
<li>The results can be sorted by the login time.</li>
<li>Under <strong>Verification Method</strong>, <strong>Current System</strong> stands for general login while <strong>Third Party Authentication</strong> means logging in to the supOS platform through third party authorization centers.</li>
</ul>
</ul>
