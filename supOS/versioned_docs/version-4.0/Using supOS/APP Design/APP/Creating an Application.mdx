1. Log in to supOS, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/appDesign/7.png" /> at the upper-right corner to go to the design center.
2. Select <strong>Business Designer</strong> tab, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/appDesign/16.png" />.
3. Enter information of the application, and then click <strong>OK</strong>.

<img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/appDesign/1.png" />

4. Hover over the application you just created, and then click <strong>Edit Page</strong>.

:::info
Pages might be different depending on the layout type.
:::

6. Double-click or hover over the page, and then click <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/appDesign/Snap9.png" /> at the upper-right corner to start page design.

:::info
You can add more pages to the application to realize more complicated functions.
:::

7. Click <strong>Save</strong> and then click <strong>Exit</strong> at the upper-right corner to finish design.
