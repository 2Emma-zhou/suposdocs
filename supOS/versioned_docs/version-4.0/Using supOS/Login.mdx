You can only log in to the platform after authorization.
- A notification appears on the login page 60 days before expiration to remind you.
- Within 30 days after the expiration date, the platform notifies you of a 10% reduction in online users.
- 30 days after the expiration date, only admins can access the platform.


## Logging in to Operation Center 

Access the supOS platform with default account <strong>admin</strong> and password <strong>123456</strong>, and then click <strong>Login</strong>.
:::info
- Go to <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png" /> > **User Security Management** > **User Management**, and change admin password.
- The account (admin included) will be locked out after multiple wrong attempts. You can change the number of attempts from <img src="https://wordpressfreezonex.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/platform/7.png" /> > <strong>System Management</strong> > <strong>System Configuration</strong> > <strong>Password configuration</strong>. 
:::
